rice & chicken

Some things in life just belong together—peanut butter & jelly, movies & popcorn, (you & Melody)—but nothing hits quite like rice and chicken. It’s a meal that never disappoints—fluffy rice soaking up all the rich flavors from perfectly seasoned chicken, coming together in one bite of pure comfort. If this dish were a relationship, it’d be stable, reliable, and always satisfying… unlike certain people’s texting habits.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

35 min.

instructions

step 1

Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and thyme. Sear chicken skin-side down for 4-5 minutes until golden brown. Flip and cook for another 3 minutes. Remove from pan (it’ll finish cooking later).

step 2

In the same pan, melt butter and sauté onions & garlic until fragrant (about 2 minutes). Stir in rice, toasting it for 1 minute to enhance flavor. Pour in chicken broth, salt, and turmeric/saffron (if using).

step 3

Nestle the seared chicken on top of the rice. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 25-30 minutes, until rice is fluffy and chicken is fully cooked.

step 4

Fluff the rice with a fork, plate the chicken, and enjoy the flavor-packed masterpiece you just created.

ingredients

For the Chicken:

4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (or breasts, if preferred)

1 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp salt

½ tsp black pepper

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp paprika (for color & flavor)

½ tsp thyme (optional, but makes it fancy)

For the Rice:

1 cup long-grain rice (jasmine or basmati work great)

2 cups chicken broth (for maximum flavor)

1 small onion, diced

2 cloves garlic, minced

½ tsp salt

½ tsp turmeric or saffron (optional, for color and depth)

1 tbsp butter or olive oil

tools

Large pan or skillet with lid

Tongs

Knife & cutting board

Measuring cups & spoons

Fork
