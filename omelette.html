Omelette

An omelette is what happens when eggs decide to level up—fluffy, golden, and stuffed with all the good stuff. It’s the breakfast equivalent of having your life together, even if you’re just winging it and hoping for the best. Whether it’s packed with cheese, veggies, or enough meat to scare a vegan, one thing’s for sure: it’s always better when everything comes together—kinda like how a simple call to Melody would bring some closure. Just saying.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

instructions

step 1

In a medium bowl, beat eggs until no whites remain, then season with salt, pepper, and a pinch red pepper flakes.

step 2

In a medium non-stick skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Pour in eggs and tilt pan so eggs fully cover the entire pan. As eggs start to set, use a rubber spatula to drag cooked edges into center of pan. Tilt pan to let uncooked egg fall to the edge of the pan.

step 3

Once the bottom is set, but top is still a little wet, sprinkle cheese and chives on one half of omelet. Fold other side over cheese and slide omelet onto a plate.

ingredients

2 large eggs

Kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

Pinch red pepper flakes

2 Tbsp. butter

1/4 cup shredded cheddar

2 Tbsp. freshly chopped chives

tools

Bowl

Whisker

Measuring cup

Pan

Spatula
