sandwich (grilled cheese)

A grilled cheese sandwich is the ultimate love story between bread and cheese—two simple ingredients that, when heated just right, melt into a gooey, golden-brown masterpiece. It’s the edible equivalent of a warm hug, a crispy-on-the-outside, melty-on-the-inside comfort bomb that never lets you down. Whether you're pairing it with soup, devouring it at 2 AM, or just making one because adulting is hard, the grilled cheese sandwich is proof that sometimes, the best things in life are simple... and covered in butter.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

instructions

step 1

Flatten the chicken breast slightly using a rolling pin or mallet for even cooking. Rub with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes (or up to 2 hours for more flavor).

step 2

In a medium non-stick skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Pour in eggs and tilt pan so eggs fully cover the entire pan. As eggs start to set, use a rubber spatula to drag cooked edges into center of pan. Tilt pan to let uncooked egg fall to the edge of the pan.

step 3

Heat a grill pan or outdoor grill to medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Optionally, place a slice of cheese on top in the last minute of grilling to melt it.

step 4

Spread mayonnaise (or your chosen sauce) on the bun. Place lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by the grilled chicken. Add tomato slices, onions, and any other toppings you like. Cover with the top bun and press gently.

step 5

Serve with fries, a side salad, or coleslaw.

ingredients

1 boneless, skinless chicken breast

1 burger bun (or sandwich bread of choice)

1 tbsp olive oil

½ tsp salt

½ tsp black pepper

½ tsp garlic powder

1 slice of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)

1-2 lettuce leaves

2 slices of tomato

1-2 slices of onion (optional)

1 tbsp mayonnaise or preferred sauce (e.g., honey mustard, ranch, or chipotle mayo)

tools

Frying Pan

Spatula

Butter Knife

Cutting Board
