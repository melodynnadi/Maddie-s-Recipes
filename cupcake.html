Chocolate Cupcake

Chocolate cupcakes are pure happiness in edible form—moist, fluffy, and rich enough to make your taste buds write a love letter. Whether you’re celebrating, stress-eating, or just looking for an excuse to bake, these little chocolatey wonders never disappoint. They’re like a hug, a warm blanket, and a little bit of mischief, all wrapped in a cute cupcake liner. And Maddie, if you love chocolate men as much as you love chocolate cupcakes, maybe it’s time to give Melody a call—he's waiting

prep time

15 min.

bake time

20 min.

instructions

step 1

Call Melody. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a cupcake pan with liners.

step 2

In a bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk melted butter and sugar until smooth. Add egg, vanilla extract, and buttermilk, mixing until combined. Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with the hot coffee. Stir until smooth.

step 3

Divide batter evenly into cupcake liners, filling about ⅔ full. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let them cool completely before frosting.

step 4

Eat immediately, or share—if you’re feeling generous.

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

½ cup cocoa powder (the good stuff for extra richness)

¾ tsp baking soda

½ tsp baking powder

¼ tsp salt

½ cup unsalted butter (melted)

¾ cup sugar

1 large egg

½ cup buttermilk

½ cup hot coffee or hot water (enhances the chocolate flavor)

tools

Mixing bowls

Whisk & spatula

Cupcake pan & liners

Oven

Electric mixer

Piping bag (optional)

Melody
