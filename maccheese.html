mac & cheese

Mac and cheese isn’t just food—it’s a mood, a vibe, a whole experience. Whether you’re making it from scratch like a true kitchen legend or secretly still craving the boxed stuff (no shame), one thing is certain: cheese + pasta = happiness. It’s creamy, cheesy, and comforting enough to make you forget all your problems—well, except for the fact that you haven't called Melody.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

instructions

step 1

Boil a pot of salted water and cook the pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside (but don’t let it get cold—nobody likes sad, dry pasta).

step 2

In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly (this makes the sauce thick and creamy). Gradually add milk, whisking constantly until smooth. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened. Stir in salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.

step 3

Remove the pan from heat and stir in the shredded cheese until melted and smooth.

step 4

Add the cooked pasta to the cheese sauce and stir until fully coated in melty goodness.

step 5

Devour immediately while the cheese is at peak gooey perfection. Add extra toppings if you’re feeling fancy—crispy bacon, hot sauce, breadcrumbs, or even more cheese (because why not?).

ingredients

2 cups elbow macaroni (or any pasta shape you love)

2 tbsp butter

2 tbsp all-purpose flour

2 cups milk (whole milk for extra creaminess)

2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar is classic, but mix it up!)

½ tsp salt

½ tsp black pepper

½ tsp paprika (optional) (adds a little depth)

½ tsp garlic powder (optional) (because garlic makes everything better)

tools

Large pot

Saucepan

Whisk

Wooden spoon or spatula

Grater
