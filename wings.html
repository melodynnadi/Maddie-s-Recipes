wings (lemon pepper)

Lemon pepper wings are proof that sometimes, all you need in life is the perfect balance of zesty, peppery, and crispy. These golden beauties start as humble chicken wings but transform into flavor bombs once coated in a tangy lemon-pepper seasoning that wakes up your taste buds like a motivational speaker at 6 AM. Whether baked, fried, or air-fried, they deliver a crunch that echoes through the soul and a citrusy kick that makes you question why you ever settled for plain wings. Eat them with ranch, blue cheese, or just straight-up confidence—either way, your fingers will be licking themselves. It is also Melody's favorite flavor so make sure to give him a call before you begin.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

instructions

step 1

Pat the chicken wings dry with a paper towel (this helps them crisp up better). In a large bowl, toss the wings with olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika until evenly coated.

step 2

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a wire rack. Arrange wings in a single layer and bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through.

step 3

In a large bowl, mix melted butter, lemon juice, lemon zest, and lemon pepper seasoning. Toss the hot wings in the mixture until fully coated.

step 4

Plate the wings and serve with ranch, blue cheese, or extra lemon wedges. Try not to eat them all in one sitting (or do—no judgment)

ingredients

2 lbs chicken wings (drums and flats separated)

2 tbsp olive oil (or melted butter for extra flavor)

1 ½ tsp salt

1 tsp black pepper

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp onion powder (optional)

1 tsp paprika (for color and mild smokiness)

1 tbsp lemon zest (freshly grated for the best flavor)

2 tbsp lemon juice

2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning

1 tbsp butter

tools

Bowl

Oven

Tongs

Grater
